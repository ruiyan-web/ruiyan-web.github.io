About me

Welcome to my personal website. I am a postdoctoral fellow (senior research associate) in the Department of Computer Science at University of Oxford, working with Prof. Marta Kwiatkowska. Before joining University of Oxford, I obtained my PhD degree in Department of Automation at Tsinghua University. My PhD advisor was Prof. Yisheng Zhong. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Automation from Beihang University in 2015. My current research focuses on game theory, probabilistic verification and synthesis, reach-avoid differential games and multi-agent reinforcement learning.
